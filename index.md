## About

I am an Assistant Professor of Management at Buena Vista University, where I teach courses in operations management, business leadership, agribusiness management, and introductory business. My research focuses on human capital, knowledge sharing, and how artificial intelligence is reshaping work in organizations.

Before joining academia full time, I spent over two decades in operations leadership in the petroleum retail, wholesale, and transportation industries, and I continue to serve as Board Chair of Manning Regional Healthcare Center.
