# Research

## Research Interests

My primary research interest is human capital. Human capital is a broad and not well-defined construct, and my goal is to make pieces of it more tangible. I am also interested in dyadic analysis techniques applied to leader-follower interactions, examining the relational dynamic rather than the individual perspective alone.

Current areas of interest include:

- How artificial intelligence is changing business and management across functions
- Knowledge sharing (dissertation focus)
- AI-augmented decision-making in operations and supply chain management
- Grounded theory methodology
